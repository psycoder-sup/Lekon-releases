Lekon 0.2.2

Quick Capture, fully loaded

  • Add labels, defer dates, due dates, and notes right from Quick Capture — no need to open the task afterward
  • Type @, #, and other trigger characters to pull up pickers inline while you type

Project & context deletion

  • Delete projects and contexts from Settings, with a clear choice of what happens to their tasks
  • Deleted items no longer appear in pickers

CLI

  • lekon CLI now installs to /usr/local/bin, so it works in any shell without PATH tweaks
